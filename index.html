Text Based Roleplay Tutorial

Introduction
The internet holds many different activities online for people to enjoy. One of these many activities is Roleplaying. Of course, the kind I'll be sharing with you, will be a specific kind. Text Based Roleplay. Text Based Roleplay, or TBR, is something people do as a fun escape to their daily lives. And this website, will help you learn how to jump into this lovely escape. In this tutorial, you will learn how to commit actions, have dialogue and create your own character!

Character Creation
The first topic we are going to be covering in this tutorial is Character Creation! During the entirety of this, I'll be referring to one of my Characters; Shota Uchiha, as an example. Now, when creating a character you have three core subjects to think about. Personality, Background and General actions!

Let's start with personality first, everyone has one. It makes us who we are, sometimes people can be quirky, sometimes they can be annoying! And sometimes, they can be both! Just like people, your Character has their own expansive personality. Now, when deciding how you want your Character to play, it's important to consider other things as well. Like alignment. Usually, if your Character is evil- they'll have a more sinister personality. But, that's where this tutorial comes in. When your making a character, you never want to be average. You want to experiment. So instead, try making a merciful Villain who is actually kind of nice- they are only portrayed as evil because of simple misunderstandings. In other words, try to be surprising! Think outside the box!

Secondly, we're going to discuss Background! Now, similar to English Class- when writing the story behind your character- you typically want something interesting. Keep in mind that most characters involve harsh and rather repetitive backstories. This makes boring more regular and humane stories refreshing. I myself usually choose to exclusively do this. For example, Shota Uchiha comes from the Uchiha Family in the Naruto Universe. Usually, Those within the Family have no parents. Mainly because they were killed off. Essentially, they're edgy. Shota on the other hand has his entire family intact. He's very calm, and enjoys finger painting! Take note, little hobbies with likes and dislikes can help you immensely when creating your Character!
